LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026 Privacy Policy Tivro — Chinese Learning App The short version: Tivro is a 13+ app. We collect only what we need to make the app work. We do not sell your data. We do not show targeted ads. Your learning progress is stored on your device. 1. Who We Are Tivro ("we", "us", "our") is a Chinese language learning app available on the Apple App Store. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@tivroapp.com 2. Age Requirement Tivro is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately. 3. What We Collect When you create an account, we collect: Name — the display name you choose (you don't have to use your real name) Email address — used to identify your account Sign-in provider — whether you used Google, Apple, or email/password We also store: Learning progress — words learned, streaks, XP, mastered words App preferences — your chosen HSK level, daily goal, settings We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings (beyond in-app speaking practice which is processed on-device), or any payment information. 4. How We Use Your Data To create and manage your account To save and sync your learning progress To personalise your experience (e.g. recommended HSK level) To send important account-related emails (no marketing without your consent) We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party. 5. Third-Party Services Tivro uses the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy: Firebase (Google) — account authentication, data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy → Google Sign-In — optional sign-in method. Google Privacy Policy → Sign in with Apple — optional sign-in method. Apple Privacy Policy → Apple App Store / StoreKit — in-app purchases processed by Apple. Apple does not share payment details with us. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or behavioural tracking tools. 6. Data Storage & Security Your account data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure). Your learning progress is stored primarily on your device and backed up to Firebase when you're signed in. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information. 7. Your Rights You have the right to: Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you Correct — update your name or email in the app settings Delete — delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing privacy@tivroapp.com Withdraw consent — stop using the app and request deletion at any time If you are in the UK or EU, you also have rights under UK GDPR / GDPR including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in the UK, the ICO at ico.org.uk). 8. Data Retention We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained longer. 9. Children's Privacy Tivro is rated 13+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly. 10. Cookies & Tracking The Tivro app does not use cookies. Firebase may use device identifiers for authentication purposes. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) or cross-app tracking. 11. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. 12.
